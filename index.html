Campus Connect

CampusConnect is an AI-powered platform that simplifies college and scholarship selection for students. By analyzing percentiles and preferences, it provides personalized recommendations, real-time scholarship updates, and alternative career options. The platform also connects students with mentors, ensuring informed and stress-free decision-making. With an intuitive interface and AI-driven insights, CampusConnect transforms the way students plan their academic future.
